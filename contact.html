Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Students applying for admission must have passed the previous class with a minimum of 50% marks. Transfer certificates and mark sheets from the previous school are required.

Do you offer transportation facilities?

Yes, we provide transportation services covering major areas of the city. Please contact our office for route details and availability.

What is the fee structure?

Fee structure varies by class and facilities. Please visit our office or contact our admission department for detailed fee information.

Can I schedule a campus tour?

Yes, campus tours are available by appointment. Please call us or fill out the contact form to schedule your visit.
